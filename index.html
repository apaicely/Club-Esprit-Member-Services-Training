The Member Journey

From first contact to loyal traveler — here's how guests become members.

1
Lead Generation
Community events, fairs, and promo drawings introduce potential guests to Club Esprit.
2
Marketing & Outreach
Leads are invited to attend a sales presentation in exchange for vacation incentives like cruise vouchers.
3
Sales Presentation
Guests attend in-person or virtual presentations learning about benefits, savings, and travel opportunities.
4
Quality Assurance
Contract is generated and reviewed with a manager to ensure the new member is set up for success.
5
Gifting & Fulfillment
Guests receive promotional gifts; members get additional incentives. Travel bookings begin!
6
Member Services (That's YOU!)
Ongoing support, answers, and ensuring high satisfaction for all members.

Payment Types & Billing

💳
Full Payment (FPWAC)
Credit cards, Affirm, check, Barclays, or cash. Pay in full at time of purchase.
🏦
In-House Financing (IFWAC)
Requires 20% down. Financed directly through Club Esprit with monthly payments.
🎟️
Sampler (SMPLR)
Full payments only. No financing option for sampler-level memberships.
⏳
Pender (PNDR)
Pending deals where the member hasn't reached 20% down. Not considered active until met.
📅
Club Dues
All members pay monthly dues. New members starting Jan 2026: $39.99/month.
🔄
Loan Payments
In-house financed deals make monthly payments through our loan management company.
